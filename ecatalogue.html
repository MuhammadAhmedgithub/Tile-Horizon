Our Tile Range

Glazed Vitrified Tiles

Glazed vitrified tiles are made from a blend of clay, silica, and other materials that are heated to high temperatures. They have a glossy finish and are highly durable, making them ideal for high-traffic areas.

Ceramic Tiles

Ceramic tiles are made from clay that is fired at high temperatures. They come in a variety of colors, patterns, and finishes, making them suitable for both walls and floors.

Polished Tiles

Polished tiles have a smooth, glossy surface achieved through a polishing process. They offer a sophisticated look and are commonly used in both residential and commercial spaces.
